## Domain Interests

These are areas of genuine personal curiosity and motivation — not just professional skills.
When a role intersects one of these domains, lean into that connection authentically.

### Space & Aerospace
I'm drawn to the intersection of software and space — orbital mechanics, satellite systems, mission-critical reliability. The engineering constraints (latency, radiation, power budgets) produce elegant solutions.
- Satellite operations and ground systems
- Space situational awareness
- Mission-critical software and fault tolerance
- Remote sensing and earth observation
- Launch infrastructure and operations software

### Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
Not just using AI as a tool — genuinely interested in how these systems work, how to make them reliable, and how to govern them responsibly. Already building LLM-powered tooling (this job search pipeline, RAG systems, AI governance frameworks).
- LLM application development and evaluation
- AI safety, alignment, and governance
- ML infrastructure and MLOps
- Applied NLP and information extraction
- AI-assisted developer tooling
- Responsible AI deployment

### Nature, Outdoors & Environmental Tech
Happiest when technology serves conservation or outdoor access. Hiking, trail systems, environmental monitoring — any role where the mission connects to the natural world is inherently motivating.
- Environmental monitoring and sensor networks
- Conservation technology and wildlife tracking
- Climate tech and sustainability platforms
- GIS, mapping, and spatial data systems
- Outdoor recreation platforms and trail tech

### Health & Wellness Technology
Interested in tech that improves human health outcomes — especially where security and data privacy are table stakes (HIPAA, health data governance).
- Health data platforms and interoperability (FHIR, HL7)
- Digital health and telemedicine infrastructure
- Wearable and IoT health devices
- Mental health and wellness applications
- Health data privacy and security (HIPAA compliance)
- Clinical trial and research data platforms

Proposed internal LibreChat deployment backed by AWS Bedrock to give the team a data-sovereign AI chat interface — same productivity gains as public chat tools, without sensitive context leaving the cloud boundary we already control. Rationale was concrete: engineers were pasting ticket content, log snippets, and draft policies into consumer chat UIs because the productivity win was real and the official answer was "do not." That is the wrong equilibrium. LibreChat on Bedrock closes the gap: familiar UX, model choice across Anthropic and others, data stays in-account under existing IAM and audit. Framing it as enablement rather than restriction made it a policy conversation security could win instead of lose.

Great Wolf Resorts had just been acquired by Blackstone, and PCI compliance and auditability became immediate priorities overnight. The security team was four people, but the CTO's mantra — "everyone in the company is in SecOps" — gave us organizational reach. We ran phishing campaigns, deeper security audits on internal tooling, and constant user education. It wasn't just checkbox compliance; it was building a security-aware culture in a hospitality company where security had never been the core business. I pioneered the Checkmarx SAST integration into our CI/CD pipelines, embedding security gates directly into the build process rather than bolting audits on after the fact. That experience shaped how I think about compliance: it works when it's woven into the workflow, not layered on top as theater that looks good in standups but has more holes than Swiss cheese in practice.

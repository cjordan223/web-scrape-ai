At Great Wolf Resorts, I managed endpoint operations across 10,000+ devices on a hybrid Azure tenant using AutoMox for script deployment. Assets were grouped by OU, so targeting was straightforward — attach a PowerShell or Bash script, get results back quickly. I was lucky to work with an intelligent server infrastructure team that maintained solid patching schedules; my role was keeping the ship running and handling the targeted hunts when a specific vulnerability or asset needed attention. GWR had just been acquired by Blackstone, so PCI compliance and auditability became immediate priorities. The CTO's mantra was "everyone in the company is in SecOps" — small four-person security team, but organizational buy-in that made the work possible.
